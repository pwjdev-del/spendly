# Strategic Roadmap & Action Plan

Turning analysis into execution.

## 1. The 90-Day Attack Plan

### Month 1: Quick Wins (Fix the Leaks)
**Goal**: Stop churn and fix "Critical" experience gaps.
- *Focus*: Performance tuning, UI polish, Onboarding friction.
- *Metric*: Day-1 Retention.

### Month 2: Parity & Core Value (Close the Gaps)
**Goal**: Neutralize competitor advantages.
- *Focus*: "Table Stakes" features identified in Gap Analysis.
- *Metric*: Activation Rate (Feature usage).

### Month 3: Differentiation (The "Killer Feature")
**Goal**: Give them a reason to switch.
- *Focus*: Implementing the "Innovation" ideas (Outside-the-box).
- *Metric*: Net Promoter Score (NPS) / Virality.
